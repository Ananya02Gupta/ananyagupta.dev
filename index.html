About Me

I am a passionate and dedicated Front-End Developer with a strong interest in creating visually appealing, responsive, and user-friendly web interfaces.Currently pursuing my Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA), I am continuously learning and improving my technical skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern web design practices. I enjoy transforming ideas into real,functional websites and believe that clean design and smooth user experience are just as important as strong code structure. I have worked on multiple projects including a responsive Zerodha website clone, a JavaScript-based To-Do List application with local storage, and a personal portfolio website. These projects have helped me gain hands-on experience in responsive layouts, UI design, basic animations, and real-world problem solving. I am highly motivated, detail-oriented, and always eager to explore new technologies that enhance web performance and design. My goal is to grow as a professional web developer, contribute to meaningful projects, and build digital experiences that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. I am a quick learner, a team-oriented individual, and someone who takes pride in writing clean, maintainable code while continuously striving to improve my skills.
