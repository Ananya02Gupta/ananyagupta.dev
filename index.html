I am a passionate and dedicated Front-End Developer with a strong interest in creating visually appealing, responsive, and user-friendly web interfaces.Currently pursuing my Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA), I am continuously learning and improving my technical skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern web design practices.
